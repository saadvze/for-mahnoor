ماہنور — چاند کی روشنی

From Saad  ·  With everything I have

Scroll
I · Wonder

Sometimes I wonder — well, quite often to be honest — "How can someone like you really exist? Or is it just my delusion, my imaginary and fantasized figure of how a woman should be."

II · Strength

You are the most delicate and yet strongest woman I've ever met. The way you handle everything… what really can be more perfect than that?

III · Eyes

I fall in love with you every day, and every day it feels like I'm falling for the first time. The newness, the butterflies, the nervousness — the excitement to look into your eyes and see myself there… Ohh, eyes —

تمہاری آنکھیں کہتی ہیں، حصارِ ذات سے نکلو
تمنّا میری بن جاؤ، شبِ برباد سے نکلو
IV · Presence

You understand me better than anyone. You're always there whenever I feel the need of having someone — and that someone is also you, by the way. I don't even generalise my needs or wants now. It's you. Just You.

V · Hand

When you hold my hand, it feels like I'm the luckiest man on earth — the most wealthy, the most healthy, the most happy being ever. Precisely speaking —

میرے ہاتھ میں، تیرا ہاتھ ہو
ساری جنتیں میرے ساتھ ہوں
VI · Vision

If I could give you one thing in life, I would give you the ability to see yourself through my eyes — maybe then you would realise how special you are to me, how nothing goes right without you, how even the worst seems less worst when you're by my side.

VII · Possession

Yes, I do get jealous. Maybe too much — but you tell me. When you meet a person you always searched for, would you be able to see them with anyone else? Would you let anyone get anywhere near them?

اپنی مُٹھی میں چُھپا کر کسی جُگنو کی طرح
ہم تیرے نام کو چُپکے سے پڑھا کرتے ہیں
VIII · Mine

I wish I could hide you — hide you from this world, from everyone. So that no one could take you away from me. So that you would exist only for me.

IX · Purpose

You make me a better man — a man I've always desired to become, yet I couldn't, because you were not there. But now that you are, your existence motivates me to be the best version of myself. Do you want to know why I get so protective, so possessive? Because if I lose you — I swear I'd lose myself too.

تجھ تک میں بہت دِل دُکھا کر پہنچا ہوں
دعا کر مجھے کوئی بدعا نہ لگے

To me, every day is yours. Every hour is yours.
Every minute is yours. Every second is yours.

Stay. Stay with me. Stay with us.
You are my angel — my Before Anyone Else. Literally.
